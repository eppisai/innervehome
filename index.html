Themes

Blockchain

Blockchain is a distributed digital ledger system where transactions of various types (i.e. not only monetary) between parties are recorded redundantly in a multiple of databases which are slow but secure.

Data Science

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the branch of computer sciences that emphasizes the development of intelligence machines, thinking and working like humans. For example, speech recognition, problem-solving, learning and planning

Automation

Mission to help developers automate repetitive processes and to increase employee productivity. A world of devices connected by internet opens up opportunities for us that were previously unthinkable.

Smart City

We are letting the participants to take an un-orthodox approach for fast-paced growth and submit a development plan for betterment of the cities. This will let the participants to understand their challenges and by engaging with experts to get insights and solutions for a smarter way of developing city.

Cyber Security

Develop next generation security apps right from SCRATCH or based on the -CONTROL: A cryptographic Digital Control Platform. Participants will be provided access to -SDK and -ADAPTER with API with documentation which can help build security apps 2.0.

Heath Care

Looking at Healthcare in a completely new way - focusing on earlier, pre-symptomatic disease detection and prevention, instead of late diagnosis - helping clinicians access more information and intervene sooner with targeted treatments so their patients can lead longer, fuller lives.
